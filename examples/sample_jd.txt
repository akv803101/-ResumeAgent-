Senior Data Analyst — D2C Growth Team
Company: BrandCo | Location: Remote (US) | Full-Time

About the Role
BrandCo is a fast-growing D2C brand doing $200M+ in annual revenue. We're looking for a Senior Data Analyst to join our Growth team and help us make smarter decisions across acquisition, retention, and LTV optimization.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports that track core growth KPIs (CAC, LTV, churn, ROAS)
- Partner with Marketing, Product, and Finance teams to provide data-driven recommendations
- Design and analyze A/B tests across email, paid media, and on-site experiences
- Develop predictive models for customer segmentation and churn prediction
- Own the data pipeline for marketing attribution from raw event data to insight

Required Skills
- 4+ years of experience in data analytics or data science
- Advanced SQL (window functions, CTEs, query optimization)
- Python (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn) — 3+ years
- Experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake preferred)
- Proficiency in BI tools (Looker, Tableau, or Power BI)
- Experience with A/B testing and statistical significance
- Strong stakeholder communication and presentation skills

Preferred Skills
- Experience with dbt for data transformation
- Familiarity with Airflow or similar pipeline orchestration
- D2C or e-commerce domain experience
- Experience with Shopify or similar e-commerce platforms

What We Offer
- Competitive salary ($130,000–$160,000)
- Remote-first culture
- Equity participation
- Generous PTO + health benefits
